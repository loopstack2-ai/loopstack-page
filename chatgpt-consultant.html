What ChatGPT Can Do

What can ChatGPT actually do for your business?

Beyond the hype, there are concrete, reliable use cases where LLMs like ChatGPT deliver measurable value. Here's what we see working consistently in production.

Customer support

A GPT-powered chatbot trained on your documentation, FAQs, and product knowledge. Handles tier-1 questions instantly, 24/7. Routes anything complex to a human with full context already captured.

Document extraction

Feed in invoices, contracts, applications, or emails and get structured data out. LLMs understand context that regex and templates miss — variable formats, scanned documents, mixed layouts.

Internal knowledge base

Staff ask a question in plain English, the system searches your documentation, policies, and procedures, and returns a direct answer. Works better than search and doesn't require everyone to know where things are filed.

Email drafting and triage

Inbound emails classified, prioritised, and responded to with AI-drafted replies for human review. Cuts email handling time significantly for teams with high inbound volume.

Lead qualification

Inbound enquiries analysed and scored automatically. Urgent or high-value leads routed immediately. Everyone else gets a personalised, accurate response that keeps them engaged while your team prioritises properly.

Meeting summaries and actions

Transcripts turned into structured summaries, action items extracted, owners assigned, and tasks created in your project management tool automatically — minutes after the call ends.

Common questions about ChatGPT consulting

What does a ChatGPT consultant do?−
A ChatGPT consultant helps businesses integrate large language models into their workflows and products. This means designing the system, writing effective prompts, building the API connections and surrounding infrastructure, testing with real data, and deploying to production. LoopStack provides end-to-end LLM implementation — not just advice — for UK businesses.
What can ChatGPT do for a business?+
The most reliable business use cases for ChatGPT and LLMs are: customer support automation, document data extraction, internal knowledge base Q&A, email triage and drafting, lead qualification, and meeting summarisation. The key is embedding the LLM into your actual workflow rather than using it as a standalone tool people manually interact with.
What's the difference between ChatGPT and Claude?+
ChatGPT (GPT-4o) excels at conversational tasks and broad general reasoning. Claude has a larger context window and handles long documents and complex multi-step instructions particularly well. Both are capable for most business use cases. LoopStack selects the right model based on your specific task — document length, accuracy requirements, cost, and integration needs. We're not tied to either vendor.
Is ChatGPT safe to use with business data?+
Using the OpenAI API (not the ChatGPT website) means your data is not used for training by default. For sensitive data — personal information, legal documents, financial records — we implement appropriate safeguards: input sanitisation, PII redaction before API calls, data minimisation, and private or on-premise deployment where required. We can also work with GDPR-compliant EU-hosted infrastructure.
