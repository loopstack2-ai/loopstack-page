The Problem

Managing compliance in a spreadsheet is how letting agents get fined.

A portfolio of 50 properties has over 150 active expiry dates to track — gas safety certificates, EICRs, EPCs, deposit schemes, Right to Rent checks, tenancy renewals. One oversight can trigger a fine of up to £30,000.

Most letting agents are doing this manually: shared spreadsheets, calendar reminders, Post-it notes. It works until it doesn't — and when it fails, it fails expensively.

And that's before the Renters' Rights Act 2025 rewrote the rulebook. Fixed-term tenancies gone. Section 21 abolished. New mandatory obligations landing on your desk whether you're ready or not.
