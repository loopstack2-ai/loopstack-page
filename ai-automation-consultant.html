01

Map your processes

Before we write a single line of code, we spend time understanding how your business actually operates — not how you think it does. We identify where time is lost, where errors creep in, and which bottlenecks are genuinely addressable with automation.

Our Approach

Honest consulting, not technology theatre

A lot of "AI consulting" is really just vendors selling platforms they earn commission on, wrapped in strategy language. We don't work that way.

We choose tools based on what's right for your specific workflow and your existing software stack — not what pays us the most. Sometimes the right tool is Make. Sometimes it's n8n. Sometimes it's a custom API integration with no third-party platform at all.

We're also honest about what AI can't do. Not every process should be automated. Not every problem is an automation problem. If the discovery call reveals that what you actually need is a better process rather than a technical solution, we'll tell you that.

FAQ

Common questions about AI automation consulting

What does an AI automation consultant do?−
An AI automation consultant maps your existing business processes, identifies repetitive or manual tasks that can be automated, and builds AI-powered systems to handle them. This includes selecting tools (Make, Zapier, n8n, OpenAI, etc.), building integrations between your software, testing the workflows, and training your team on the finished system. The output is a working automation you own outright — not a strategy deck.
How much does AI automation consulting cost in the UK?+
LoopStack charges £997 for a single workflow project, £2,497 for a full automation suite with AI components, and £799/month for ongoing retainer work. All prices are fixed — no hourly billing, no scope creep. Full documentation is included on every project.
How long does implementation take?+
Simple automations can be live within 48 hours of scoping. Starter projects (single workflow) take 5–7 working days. Growth projects with multiple integrations and AI components take 2–4 weeks. You get a firm delivery date after the free discovery call.
What business processes can be automated?+
Common processes we automate: invoice and document processing, lead qualification and CRM data entry, customer support routing, internal reporting, email triage, onboarding workflows, inventory updates, and data sync between applications. If a human is doing the same task repeatedly, it's almost always automatable.
Do I own what gets built?+
Yes. Everything belongs to you — all workflows, API connections, credentials, and documentation. No vendor lock-in. You can maintain it yourself, hand it to another provider, or continue with our retainer. That choice is always yours.
