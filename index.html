FAQ

Common questions

Can't find your answer? Drop us a message and we'll get back within a few hours.

What is an AI automation consultant?
An AI automation consultant assesses your business processes, identifies where AI can save time and money, then builds and deploys the technical solution. That means mapping workflows, selecting the right tools, building integrations, testing everything, and training your team. LoopStack does this end-to-end for UK businesses — no handoffs to junior developers, no disappearing after kickoff.
How much does AI automation cost in the UK?
LoopStack charges £997 for a single workflow automation (Starter), £2,497 for a full multi-step suite with AI components (Growth), and £799/month for ongoing retainer work. All projects are fixed-price — no hourly billing, no scope creep surprises. Full documentation is included on every project.
How quickly can you implement AI automation?
We can deploy a first working automation within 48 hours of scoping. Starter projects take 5–7 working days. Growth projects with AI components take 2–4 weeks. You get a firm delivery date after the free discovery call — not a range, an actual date.
Do I need technical knowledge?
None at all. Our clients are mostly non-technical founders, operations directors, and business owners. You understand your processes — we handle the AI, the code, and the integrations. Everything we build is documented in plain English so your team can understand what's running.
What AI tools and platforms do you use?
Make (formerly Integromat), Zapier, n8n, Airtable, Notion, HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, OpenAI GPT-4, Anthropic Claude, and custom API integrations. The choice depends on your existing stack — we don't push tools we get commissions on, we use what's right for the job.
Can small businesses benefit from AI automation?
Absolutely — and often more than larger ones, because manual tasks consume a bigger proportion of a small team's time. Common wins for UK SMEs: automated invoicing, lead follow-up sequences, document processing, and AI customer support chatbots. The £997 Starter package is built specifically for businesses wanting to automate one high-impact process without a huge upfront investment.
What happens after the project is delivered?
You own everything — all workflows, API credentials, and documentation belong to you. There's a video walkthrough handover and support period included (2 weeks on Starter, 4 weeks on Growth). Monthly retainer packages are available for ongoing maintenance and new builds, but there's no pressure or obligation to continue after the project ends.
