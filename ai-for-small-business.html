Which automation pays back fastest for an SME?

Not every automation delivers the same return. Based on what we've seen across 40+ UK clients, these three give the fastest payback for small businesses:

1. Invoice processing — ROI in 2-4 weeks

If someone on your team spends 2-3 hours per week entering supplier invoices into accounting software, that's 100-150 hours per year. AI invoice extraction costs £997 (Starter package) and typically saves 90% of that time. At £25/hour blended staff cost, that's roughly £2,500-3,750 in annual savings against a £997 investment. The automation pays for itself in 6-8 weeks.

2. Lead follow-up — ROI in 4-6 weeks

Manual lead follow-up is the #1 complaint from our SME clients. A lead comes in via web form, someone has to check it, craft a response, and send it. If you receive 20+ enquiries per week, automation that responds within 60 seconds and routes hot leads to your phone can recover 3-5 hours of staff time per week while capturing sales that would otherwise go cold.

3. Email triage — ROI in 6-8 weeks

AI email triage reads incoming messages, categorises them (urgent, standard, marketing, spam), and either drafts a response or routes to the right person. For a business receiving 50+ emails per day, this can save 4-6 hours of weekly sorting time across the team.
